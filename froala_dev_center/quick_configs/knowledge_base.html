How to Reset Your Password

Article ID: KB-0123 | Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Overview

This guide will walk you through the process of resetting your account password. The process typically takes less than 5 minutes.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password" below the login form
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for the reset link
  5. Create a new password

Troubleshooting

If you don't receive the reset email within 5 minutes:

  • Check your spam folder
  • Verify you entered the correct email address
  • Contact support if the issue persists
