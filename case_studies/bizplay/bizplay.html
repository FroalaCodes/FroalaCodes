About Bizplay

Bizplay launched in 2011 with a clear mission: make digital signage easy, scalable, and accessible for everyone. The company built a cloud-based platform that lets teams create, schedule, and manage dynamic screen content in just a few clicks.

From menus and announcements to promotions and live data, Bizplay makes it simple to keep every message fresh, visually engaging, and ready to drive action.

Their Mission: Help every organization tell better stories, engage audiences effectively, and get more value from every screen.

Who uses the platform

Bizplay serves organizations that depend on clear, timely, and compelling on-screen communication across retail, hospitality, workplaces, education, and public venues.

The Challenge

Content creation and management power Bizplay's entire strategy. By giving teams fast, intuitive tools to build and update content, they keep users agile and consistent across all screens and locations.

Before implementing Froala, the platform had limited formatting capabilities:

  • Plain text entry only, suitable for simple title blocks and paragraphs
  • Users wanted more formatting options like tables and emojis
  • The team needed a WYSIWYG editor that could integrate smoothly into their CMS
  • Required an editor that matched the familiar experience users expect from standard text editors
